Springtime in Ann Arbor is a beautiful time. Even though it can be chilly, it is nice to walk my dog Bacon. This site includes some pictures I have taken on these walks.

Sometimes we walk around the neighborhood, other times we go into the woods. Ann Arbor also has beautiful lakes and rivers including the Huron River Watertrail and the Argo Park Cascades.
